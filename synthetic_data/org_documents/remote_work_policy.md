# Meridian Technologies: Hybrid Work Policy

**Effective Date: May 1, 2026**
**Policy Owner: Human Resources**
**Last Updated: March 2026**

---

## Policy Statement

Meridian Technologies is committed to a hybrid work model that balances collaboration, flexibility, and employee well-being. This updated policy reflects our post-reorganization operating model and replaces the previous interim remote work guidelines issued in 2024.

## Standard Expectations

Effective May 1, 2026, the standard expectation for most roles at Meridian is a minimum of **three days per week in the office**, with the specific days determined collaboratively between each employee and their direct manager.

### Guiding Principles

1. **Collaboration days are intentional.** In-office days should prioritize meetings, team working sessions, and activities that benefit from in-person interaction. Managers should avoid scheduling in-office days that employees spend entirely in virtual meetings they could attend from home.

2. **Flexibility within structure.** The three-day minimum applies at the weekly level. Employees and managers may adjust specific days week to week based on project needs, as long as the overall cadence is maintained.

3. **Trust is the foundation.** This policy is built on mutual trust between employees and the organization. We trust employees to manage their time and location in ways that serve both their productivity and the team's needs.

## Role Categories

| Category | In-Office Expectation | Examples |
|----------|----------------------|----------|
| Collaborative Roles | 3+ days/week | Most engineering, product, and business teams |
| Client-Facing Roles | As needed for client engagement, minimum 2 days/week | Sales, customer success, professional services |
| Fully Remote Eligible | As approved, minimum 1 day/month for team events | Specific roles identified by VP-level approval |
| On-Site Required | 5 days/week | Facilities, lab operations, physical security |

## Exceptions Process

Employees who believe their role qualifies for a different arrangement than the standard three-day minimum may request an exception through the following process:

1. **Submit a request** to your direct manager with a written rationale
2. **Manager review** within five business days, considering team needs, role requirements, and collaboration patterns
3. **HR consultation** for any exception lasting longer than 90 days
4. **VP approval** required for permanent remote arrangements

All exceptions are reviewed quarterly to ensure they remain appropriate as team structures evolve.

## Workspace Support

Meridian provides the following to support hybrid work:

- **Office:** Dedicated desks for employees in-office 3+ days/week; hot-desking options for others
- **Home:** A one-time $500 home office stipend for new hires; ergonomic equipment requests through IT
- **Technology:** Company-issued laptop, VPN access, collaboration tools (Slack, Zoom, shared drives)
- **Commuting:** Pre-tax commuter benefits and subsidized parking

## Transition Period

During the organizational restructuring (May through July 2026), we recognize that some teams will be in flux. Managers should exercise judgment during this period, particularly for employees whose team assignments or reporting lines are changing. The intent of this policy is to support productive work, not to create unnecessary stress during a period of transition.

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers play a critical role in making hybrid work successful. Responsibilities include:

- Establishing clear team norms about which activities are best suited for in-office versus remote days
- Ensuring that remote employees are not disadvantaged in meetings, decisions, or visibility
- Having regular one-on-one conversations about how the arrangement is working for each team member
- Flagging concerns to HR rather than making unilateral changes to the policy

## Employee Responsibilities

Employees are expected to:

- Communicate proactively with their manager about schedule adjustments
- Be responsive and available during core business hours regardless of location
- Maintain a professional and functional home workspace
- Report any sustained challenges with the hybrid model to their manager or HR
